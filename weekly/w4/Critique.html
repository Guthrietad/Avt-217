Project Restaurant Critiques

SweetGreen

This website is very easy to navigate especially from home page. The homage has accessibility to all tabs through nav located on top and bottom of page. This makes it very simple for the user to navigate to their endpoint without having to search through multiple pages. This website boasts a two grid layout. Also the contrast between color combinations creates good hierarchy.

Bartaco

When looking at this page the layout is very simple and easily navigable. On the home page, they offer direct links to two of their Main facets the “order now” and locations. In addition, there is a left centered nav with all respective links to the websites pages. The page is laid out on a four grid structure. Also color themes are very uniform and make the page more appealing.

Tashas

Upon arriving on this page, you are greeted with a video including social media links and a nav dropdown with all the respective pages on the site. This website is very easy to navigate and sleek with the dropdown navigation. Site is very illustrative with minimal text and simple 2-3 grid layout. At all stages throughout website navigation the user has access to social meida links as well as nav dropdown. Due to the dropdown nature it isn’t too intrusive and keeps the aesthetic minimal.

Green Lemon

Upon arriving at home page it is evident that they make it very easy for you to order online. There are multiple ways to access online ordering from home page. When looking throughout the pages they have a three grid layout consistent throughout. Social media presence is also accessible from the home page. This page boast a very vibrant color way with the main links being displayed in the most vibrant colors.

Wagamama

This page is quite interactive. First thing I noticed was the video on homepage with a nav gradient dropdown upon hover. This make the page look very slick. The red white and black color combination is consistent throughout and creates good hierarchy. This is a very bold website with the minimal color way as well. This page boasts a 2-3grid layout.
